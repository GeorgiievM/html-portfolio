About Me

Hi, I’m Martin Georgiev — a creative media designer, videographer, and storyteller based in Austria, originally from Macedonia. I specialize in crafting powerful visual content that not only looks good but connects emotionally and leaves a lasting impact.

Whether it’s through cinematic video production, eye-catching motion graphics, or capturing breathtaking aerial footage with my drone, I bring a sharp eye for detail and a passion for meaningful storytelling to every project I take on. From weddings and branded content to creative social media campaigns — I don’t just shoot and edit; I shape moments into experiences.

I’m also diving into the world of web development — currently learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to bring my creative skills into digital spaces. While I’m still at the beginning of this journey, I’m excited to blend design with interactivity and build modern, responsive websites.

With a background in graphic design and audiovisual media, plus hands-on experience in Adobe Creative Suite, DaVinci Resolve, After Effects, and more, I combine technical skill with a human touch. I also hold a certified drone license, so I can elevate projects — literally and creatively.

When I’m not behind a camera or coding something new, I’m constantly learning, exploring tools, and pushing creative boundaries.

Let’s bring your vision to life — with style, purpose, and impact.
